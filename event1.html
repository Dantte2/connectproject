tue 9.9.2025 at 8–9 | Webinar

How can a small business strengthen its financing opportunities?

The webinar is targeted at growth-oriented micro-enterprises and entrepreneurs who have the need and desire to strengthen their fundability. According to studies, obtaining financing is currently one of the biggest challenges for small businesses.

The webinar is practical. It goes through, with concrete examples, how a business can strengthen its internal operations and how this affects its fundability. Participants will also be given a couple of reflection tasks.

Expert

Jorma Sonninen, an experienced expert in financing and micro-entrepreneurship, entrepreneur, and business developer.

Jorma has a background of 15 years in banking. He has conducted hundreds of credit negotiations and made hundreds of credit decisions. Both sides of the counter are familiar to him, and today he paves a concrete path for business development.

Subsequently, he has worked as a micro-entrepreneur for over 20 years, has participated in numerous credit negotiations as a customer, and has also developed the business operations of dozens of small companies together with his spouse, Sari Sonninen.
